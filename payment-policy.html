Payment Policy

At AR Resin Art, we require full payment before beginning work on your custom resin artwork. This policy ensures that we can dedicate the necessary resources and time to create your unique piece.

Payment Methods

We accept payments through credit cards, PayPal, or bank transfers. Please ensure that your payment is completed before the artwork production starts.

Full Payment Requirement

Before any work begins, you must pay the full amount for your custom resin art, including any applicable shipping fees. This policy is in place to cover the costs of materials and labor involved in crafting your personalized piece.

Failure to Pay

If payment is not received within 7 days of placing your order, AR Resin Art reserves the right to cancel the order and retain any deposits or fees already paid.
